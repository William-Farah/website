About Us

Queensland Compassionate Services is a disability support services provider based in Boondall, Queensland.

The company was established with the sole aim of offering support that always puts participants first.

At Queensland Compassionate Services we provide a range of disability services. In doing so, we aim to make a difference in the lives of those living with a disability. The goal is to make each of our participants’ lives easier, fairer, and more involved.

Mission Statement

We are a family-owned NDIS service provider located in Brisbane, Australia.

We are passionate about supporting people with disabilities to achieve their goals by providing high quality & effective disability services where every person we care for matters.

We care about people with disabilities and we will walk the extra mile to provide the services they require for a dignified, fulfilling life.

Value Statement

Queensland Compassionate Services values are centred around our staff, participants, and their families or guardians.

  Respect - We respect people’s decisions, opinions, and views

  Honesty - We strive to be trustworthy and truthful.

  Privacy and Confidentiality - This is of the utmost importance as outlined in our Privacy and Confidentiality Policy and Procedure.

  Development and Improvement - We will identify, with your help, areas for development and improvement projects. We always work to improve our services and uphold service standards. Your feedback is always valued.

  Safe and Harmonious working environment - We offer a workplace that provides support, guidance, and acceptance to staff and participants.

  Passion - Every member of staff is passionate about the work that they do and considers all our participants a part of our Queensland Compassionate Services family.

  Teamwork - we value people achieving results through teamwork

Our Vision

Our work is grounded in our compassionate and deep care for our clients, and our vision is to expand so that we are able to provide a one-stop-shop for people with disabilities seeking support services through the NDIS.

What Q.C Services Offers

Disability Core Services

Disability Support Coordination/ Psychosocial

Recovery Coaching
We deliver high quality community access and domestic assistance services including cleaning that are consistent with the Human Services Quality Standards and the principles and service philosophy of the Queensland Compassionate Services, such as:

Complete domestic duties including cleaning, housework, meal preparation and other light domestic assistance.

Be a companion, lifestyle support person for our clients in their homes.

Transferring in and out of bed (if needed), chairs and providing other mobility assistance.

Showering (if needed)

Grooming, shaving and doing hair.

Toileting (including changing catheter bags and supporting bowel care)

Community access such as driving to appointments and social activities.

Our support coordination team provide level 1 and 2 of Support Coordination and Psychosocial Recovery Coaching.

When the NDIS plan provides Support Coordination or Psychosocial Recovery Coaching funding, our experienced team will support the participant and their family with:

Understanding the NDIS and the plan itself.

Implementing the NDIS plan.

Providing a list of different suitable service providers to choose from.

Linking with the chosen service providers while aligning the expenditure with the funding throughout the plan life cycle.

Mentoring and monitoring the providers’ service agreements assuring that they reflect the participant’s needs according to the NDIS regulations.

Providing reports to NDIA on outcomes prior to review.

Helping the participant preparing themselves to their NDIS plan review meeting.
