Our Commitment to You

We descend from a Culturally and linguistically diverse (CALD) background with wealth of multicultural experience and emotional intelligence.

While we were blessed to integrate with our new society, we perfectly understand challenges facing people with CALD backgrounds and especially those living with disabilities.

Queensland Compassionate Services is a disability support services provider, established with the sole aim of offering support that always puts participants first.

At Queensland Compassionate Services we provide a range of disability services, with the aim to make a difference in the lives of those living with a disability. The goal is to make each of our participants’ lives easier, fairer, and more involved.

Mission Statement

We are a family-owned NDIS service provider in Brisbane, dedicated to empowering people with disabilities to achieve their goals.

We deliver high-quality, effective services, ensuring every person we care for matters.

Our commitment is to walk the extra mile for a dignified, fulfilling life.

Value Statement

At Queensland Compassionate Services, our values are centered on respect, honesty, and privacy.

We are committed to continuous improvement, fostering a supportive and passionate work environment, and achieving results through teamwork.

Your feedback drives our dedication to providing safe, high-quality care.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a comprehensive, one-stop-shop for NDIS support services, driven by our compassionate care and commitment to improving the lives of people with disabilities.
